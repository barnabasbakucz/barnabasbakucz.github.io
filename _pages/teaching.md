## University Name

### As Head Teaching Fellow / Instructor

**ECON 315 — Political Economy**  
*Spring 2024*  
Upper-level undergraduate course covering the political determinants of economic policy,
voting models, lobbying, and media influence. Responsible for sections, office hours,
and co-designing two problem sets.

---

### As Teaching Fellow

**ECON 210 — Behavioral Economics**  
*Fall 2023*  
Survey of departures from the standard rational-agent model: present bias, reference
dependence, social preferences, and bounded rationality. Led weekly sections of 25 students.

**ECON 101 — Principles of Economics**  
*Spring 2023, Spring 2022*  
Introductory micro and macro for non-majors. Led two sections per term.

**ECON 450 — Development Economics**  
*Fall 2022*  
Topics in growth, poverty, and field experiments. Led section and graded problem sets.
